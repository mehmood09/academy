Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you get started

Q Do I need prior programming experience?
No prior experience is required for our beginner-friendly courses. We start with fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced topics. However, for specialized courses like Data Science, basic programming knowledge is recommended.
Q What is the duration of each course?
Course duration varies by program. Frontend Development and React courses are 4 months, Full Stack Django is 5 months, and Data Science is 6 months. All courses include flexible scheduling to accommodate your lifestyle.
Q Are the courses live or pre-recorded?
We offer a hybrid approach! You'll get live interactive sessions with instructors for real-time learning and Q&A, plus access to pre-recorded lectures that you can review anytime. This gives you the best of both worlds.
Q Will I receive a certificate after completion?
Yes! Upon successful completion of the course and final project, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile to showcase your new skills.
Q Do you provide job placement assistance?
Absolutely! We offer comprehensive career support including resume building, portfolio review, mock interviews, and connections with hiring partners. Our dedicated placement team works with you until you land your desired role.
Q Can I pay in installments?
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans! You can choose to pay in monthly installments with zero interest. We also provide early bird discounts and scholarship opportunities for eligible students.
